    Zucchini

    Zucchini, also called courgette in some parts of the world

    A favorite among low-carb dieters and anyone who wants to lose weight fast

    Nutrient Overview:

    1. Particularly high vitamin A content

    2. Contains both soluble and insoluble fiber

    3. Rich in carotenoids such as lutein, zeaxanthin, and beta-carotene

    Health Benefits:

    1. Reduces the risk of constipation and symptoms of various gut disorders

    2. May lower blood pressure, cholesterol, and other risk factors for heart disease

    Broccoli

    Broccoli can be enjoyed both raw and cooked

    Raw broccoli contains almost 90% water, 7% carbs, 3% protein, and almost no fat

    Nutrient Overview:

    1. Higher in protein than most vegetables

    2. Rich in folate, potassium, manganese, iron, and vitamins C and K1

    3. Low in digestible carbs but provides a decent amount of fiber

    Health Benefits:

    1. Helps lower cholesterol and boosts eye health

    2. Promotes gut health and may reduce your risk of various diseases
