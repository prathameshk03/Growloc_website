    Romaine Lettuce

    Romaine is one of the most versatile types of lettuce.

    Flavour: It has a sweet, gentle flavour and a crisp bite that stands up well to punchy flavours.

    Nutrient Overview:

    1. Good source of beta carotene

    2. High amount of Vitamin A, B6, K and C

    3. High in minerals like calcium, phosphorus, magnesium and potassium

    Health Benefits:

    1. Improves muscle performance

    2. Helps prevent eye conditions like cataracts

    3. Maintains cardiovascular health

    Butterhead Lettuce

    Butterhead is known for its soft, buttery leaves.

    Flavour: Sweet with a mild taste.

    Nutrient Overview:

    1. Rich in Vitamin K

    2. Contains carotenoid antioxidants

    3. Contains a good amount of iron and calcium

    Health Benefits:

    1. Protects eyes from degeneration

    2. Promotes reproduction

    3. Strengthens bones

    Lollo Rossa Lettuce

    Lollo Rosso is favoured for its texture, colour, and curly edges.

    Flavour: It gives a sweet, slightly bitter, and a nutty flavour.

    Nutrient Overview:

    1. Rich in vitamins A, C

    2. Contains high amounts of folate

    3. Contains more antioxidants than regular lettuce

    Health Benefits:

    1. Improves eyesight

    2. Helps relieve asthma and allergy symptoms

    3. Improves and induces sleep.
